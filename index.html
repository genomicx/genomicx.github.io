The browser is the new lab bench.

Most bioinformatics tools require command-line experience, specific operating systems, complex dependency chains, or institutional computing resources. That leaves out students on personal laptops, clinicians who need quick answers, and researchers at under-resourced institutions.

GenomicX compiles established, peer-reviewed bioinformatics software into WebAssembly modules that run entirely in your browser. No servers to maintain. No data to upload. No barriers to entry.

We believe genomic science should be open, private, and accessible to everyone.
